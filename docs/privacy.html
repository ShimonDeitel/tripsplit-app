Privacy Policy

Tripmate Split is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server, and we do not collect, sell, or share any personal information.

The only network connection the app makes is to Apple's App Store servers to process in-app purchases, which Apple handles under its own privacy policy.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it.
